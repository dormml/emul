	To install the demos in this archive, be sure you have first
	installed V9t9 on your system.
	
	Then, go to the directory where you installed V9t9's included 
	demonstration files (usually the \DEMOS subdirectory in the V9t9 
	directory), and	enter:

	C:\V9t9\DEMOS> PKUNZIP -d 600VDEMS.ZIP

	(Substitute the appropriate filename above.)
